Jaewook Lee

I am a Post-Doctoral Researcher at Leiden University working on the Horizon Europe TransEuroWorkS project. My research examines how structural economic transformations — such as automation, globalization, and the green transition — shape public opinion, welfare states, and political behavior in advanced capitalist democracies.
